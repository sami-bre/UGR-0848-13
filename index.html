Something about Wisdom School

Wisdom school is located in Addis Ababa, 4 kilo. Addis Ababa is the sprawling capital city of Ethiopia. Wisdom School has been a part of the Addis community for over 25 years and has an outstanding heritage and reputation for dedicated teachers and effective instruction. Currently Wisdom School serves approximately 1500 students in 3 different capuses.
